Submit a Story

When you submit a story to this site, you must provide certain information. Please choose a short, but descriptive title for your story. Also, you will need to select which topic the story will be filed. Finally you have the option to write a short Introduction text and then the full story body text. The Story Body text is optional, so you do not have to fill in this field.
  • Fill in the title, it is required
  • Provide complete and accurate information
  • Double check those URLs
Field  Required Description
Title Yes The title of your story. Please make it short, but as descriptive as possible.
Topic  Yes Which topic should this story be filed?
Story Introduction (required) Yes This is your story. You can write a short 'teaser' introduction and then place the body of your story in the Story Body if you wish. Or, you can place the entire story in this field.
Story Body (optional) Yes The main body of your story. This field is optional if you decided to place the entire story in the Introduction field.
Postmode Yes Postmode determines if your post will be HTML or plain text. Generally HTML is the best option to choose. If your story will contain code examples or HTML examples, be sure to wrap them in the [code] [/code] blocks to ensure they are not translated with the story is displayed.
